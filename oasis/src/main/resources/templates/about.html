NJU Software Engineering Institute

NJU SE is a teaching and research engineering college affiliated to Nanjing University. It is the first batch of national-level model software colleges approved by the State Planning Commission and the Ministry of Education in 2002. The college now has a software engineering department, an information system engineering department, an embedded technology department, as well as a national Linux technology training and promotion center (Ministry of Education / Ministry of Science and Technology), and a national software talent international training (Nanjing) base (State Foreign Experts Affairs Bureau).
